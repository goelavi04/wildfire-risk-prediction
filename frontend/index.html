What These Metrics Mean
Accuracy Overall correct predictions out of all predictions made
Precision When model says fire — how often it is actually right
Recall Of all real fire days — how many did the model catch
F1 Score Balance between Precision and Recall
ROC-AUC 0.86 = excellent ability to separate fire vs no-fire days
